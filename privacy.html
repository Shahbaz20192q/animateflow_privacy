Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

Welcome to AutomateFlow. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect your information when you use our social media automation services, including our AI Smart Inbox, Comment-to-DM, and Sentiment Analysis tools.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect basic details such as your email address and profile information.
  • Social Media Data (via APIs): When you connect your social media accounts (e.g., Instagram, Facebook, Twitter), we receive access tokens that allow us to interact with the respective APIs. We collect public comments, direct messages, usernames, and profile metrics to facilitate your automations.
  • AI Processing Data: Text from incoming messages and comments is temporarily processed by our AI systems to categorize intent and analyze sentiment.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to operate and improve AutomateFlow. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Trigger automated direct messages based on specific keywords (Comment-to-DM).
  • Analyze and categorize your incoming messages (AI Smart Inbox).
  • Generate aggregated sentiment reports based on user engagement.
  • Ensure compliance with the developer policies of Meta, X, and other connected platforms.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. Your information is only shared in the following circumstances:

  • Platform Integrations: We transmit data back and forth to Meta, X, or other platforms to execute the actions you have authorized.
  • AI Service Providers: De-identified text from comments and DMs may be processed by third-party AI models solely for the purpose of sentiment analysis and categorization.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your connected account data only for as long as your AutomateFlow account is active.

  • Revoking Access: You can revoke AutomateFlow's access to your social media accounts at any time through the security settings of the respective platforms (e.g., Meta's App Settings).
  • Data Deletion: You may request the complete deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us directly. Upon request, all stored API tokens, automation logs, and personal details will be permanently erased from our servers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to submit a data deletion request, please contact us at: support@automateflow.com
